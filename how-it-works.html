HOW LEGISLY WORKS

From onboarding to live hearing intelligence — built for how CA lobbyists actually work.

01
DAY 1

Onboard your firm & migrate from CapitolTrack

Set up your firm account with multi-tenant architecture. Import your client bill lists, positions, tags, notes, and historical tracking data from CapitolTrack exports or CSV/Excel.

CapitolTrack Migration Wizard

Our migration tool imports your existing data so you can switch without losing any client history. User roles for firm admins, lobbyists, and client viewers.

02
AUTOMATIC

CA + Federal data pipeline starts tracking

Real-time bill ingestion from California Legislative Information and Congress.gov. Bill status, amendment history, vote records, legislator data, and committee memberships — all synced automatically.

Always current

Automated data refresh keeps your bill tracking, legislator profiles, and committee schedules up to date 24/7.

03
DAILY WORKFLOW

Manage clients, positions & stakeholders

Track bill positions (Support/Oppose/Watch/Neutral) per client. Maintain internal memos, stakeholder contacts, and coalition relationships — all linked to active bills.

Stakeholder CRM

A contact database for legislators, staff, advocates, and coalition contacts linked to your clients and their active bills.

04
ONE CLICK

Generate branded reports & share with clients

Create professional reports with your firm's logo and branding. One-click PDF export. Or give clients their own white-labeled portal with live bill status, hearing alerts, and position notes.

Client Intelligence Portal

A branded, read-only login for each client with live dashboards, hearing alerts, and a WYSIWYG builder so you control what each client sees.

05
REAL-TIME

Live hearing intelligence & AI analysis

Real-time audio capture and transcription of CA legislative hearings. AI-generated summaries, speaker identification, vote detection, bill outcome predictions, and client-specific relevance scoring.

Never miss a hearing

Searchable transcript archives, automated alerts when your clients' bills are discussed, and AI-powered key moment extraction.
