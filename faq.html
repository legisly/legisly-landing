FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Legisly.

What is Legisly? ▼

Legisly is a legislative tracking platform with real-time hearing intelligence, purpose-built for California lobbying firms. It covers both California and federal legislation with a single integrated platform.

Features include real-time bill tracking, live hearing transcription and AI analysis, client and position management, branded client reports, a white-labeled Client Intelligence Portal, regulatory tracking, budget tracking, and FPPC compliance tools.

Who is Legisly for? ▼

Legisly is purpose-built for California lobbying firms and government affairs professionals, including:

• Solo lobbyists and small lobbying firms
• Mid-size and large CA lobbying firms
• Government affairs consultancies
• Trade associations with CA legislative priorities
• Corporate government relations teams focused on Sacramento

What jurisdictions do you cover? ▼

Legisly covers California state legislation (via leginfo.legislature.ca.gov) and federal legislation (via Congress.gov). We also track CA regulatory activity through the Office of Administrative Law and federal rulemakings through the Federal Register and Regulations.gov.

Multi-state coverage (TX, NY, FL) is planned for a future phase — our primary focus is California + Federal.

Can I migrate from CapitolTrack? ▼

Yes. Legisly includes a CapitolTrack Data Migration Wizard that imports your client bill lists, positions, tags, notes, and historical tracking data from CapitolTrack exports. We also support CSV/Excel imports from other legacy systems.

Your firm can switch without losing any client history or data.

What is Live Hearing Intelligence? ▼

Legisly captures and transcribes CA legislative hearings in real-time with speaker identification and timestamp linking. Our AI then generates hearing summaries, extracts key moments, detects votes, and scores hearing relevance per client.

All hearings are archived with searchable transcripts. Federal committee hearings are also monitored where audio/video is available.

What is the Client Intelligence Portal? ▼

The Client Intelligence Portal is a white-labeled, read-only login that you give to your clients. Each client sees a live bill status dashboard, hearing alerts, position notes from their lobbyist, and a legislative calendar filtered to their issues.

The portal is branded per firm (your branding, not Legisly's), and a WYSIWYG builder lets you customize what each client sees.

Is my data secure? ▼

Absolutely. Legisly uses multi-tenant architecture with strict firm and client separation. We use bank-level encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Client positions, internal memos, and firm data are never shared between tenants.

Enterprise plans include SSO/OAuth and role-based access control. See our Security page for details.

What does FPPC compliance tracking include? ▼

Legisly tracks contributions, supports 460/496 form workflows, and maintains a fundraising activity log per client. You'll get compliance deadline alerts for FPPC filing dates so nothing slips through the cracks.

When is Legisly launching? ▼

Legisly is launching in Summer 2026, starting with California + Federal coverage. Development begins March 2026. Request a demo to get early access and provide input during development.
