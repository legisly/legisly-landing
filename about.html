About Legisly

Purpose-built for California lobbying firms.

California's legislative landscape moves fast. Every session, thousands of bills are introduced across Sacramento and Washington — each one potentially affecting your clients. Your firm needs tools that keep pace.

Legacy platforms weren't built for how modern lobbying firms actually work.

Our Mission

To give California lobbying firms the most powerful legislative intelligence platform available — with real-time hearing intelligence, AI-powered analysis, and branded client portals that make your firm indispensable.

The Problem We're Solving

Existing legislative tracking tools were built in a different era. CapitolTrack and similar platforms provide basic bill tracking, but they lack real-time hearing intelligence, AI-powered analysis, modern client portals, and the kind of automation that lets a small firm punch above its weight.

Meanwhile, your clients expect instant updates, professional branded reports, and real-time access to their bill status. The gap between what they expect and what legacy tools deliver is only growing.

Our Solution

Legisly is a legislative tracking platform with real-time hearing intelligence, purpose-built to serve California lobbying firms. We cover California and federal legislation with a single integrated platform that includes:

• Real-time CA + federal bill tracking with automated data pipelines
• Client and position management with a stakeholder CRM
• Branded client reports and a white-labeled Client Intelligence Portal
• AI assistant for bill summaries, position drafting, and impact analysis
• Live hearing transcription with AI-generated summaries and vote detection
• CA regulatory tracking, budget tracking, and FPPC compliance
• CapitolTrack data migration wizard for seamless switching

Our Values

Built for Lobbyists

Every feature is designed for how California lobbying firms actually work — not generic "legislative tracking."

Real-Time Intelligence

Live hearing transcription, instant alerts, and AI analysis — because in Sacramento, timing is everything.

Client-First Design

Branded portals, professional reports, and tools that make your firm look exceptional to every client.

Seamless Migration

Switch from CapitolTrack or any legacy system without losing your client history, positions, or data.

The Team

Legisly is built by a team with deep experience in legislative technology and AI. We understand the California legislative process, the needs of lobbying firms, and the technology required to deliver real-time intelligence at scale.

We're based in California and laser-focused on capturing the CA market.
